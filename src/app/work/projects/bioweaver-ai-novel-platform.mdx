## Overview

BioWeaver (formerly Novel Helper) is an AI-powered platform designed to help elderly individuals preserve their life stories as structured biographical narratives. Unlike generic AI writing tools, BioWeaver is purpose-built for a non-technical audience: users speak their memories into a voice recorder, and the platform handles the rest — transcription, narrative structuring, chapter organization, and polished prose generation. The result is a professionally written biography that preserves family history with dignity and accuracy.

## Key Features

- **Voice-First Input**: Optimized for elderly users who may not be comfortable typing. Record stories in natural speech — the platform handles pauses, corrections, and tangential stories gracefully.
- **Whisper Transcription**: OpenAI Whisper (via OpenRouter) transcribes voice recordings with high accuracy. Supports English and Mandarin Chinese with automatic language detection.
- **Claude Narrative Polish**: Anthropic Claude transforms raw transcription into polished prose while preserving the storyteller's voice. The AI is prompted to maintain authenticity, not to embellish.
- **Chapter Auto-Organization**: The platform detects natural story boundaries (time periods, life events, themes) and organizes content into logical chapters. Users can reorder, merge, or split chapters.
- **Biography Export**: Export the completed biography as PDF (print-ready) or EPUB (e-reader). Customizable cover page with family photos.
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: All voice recordings and transcripts are stored locally. No data is used for AI training. Designed for sensitive personal content.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Vite, TailwindCSS
- **Backend**: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy Async, PostgreSQL
- **AI**: Claude 3.5 (OpenRouter), OpenAI Whisper
- **Export**: WeasyPrint (PDF), custom EPUB generator
- **Infrastructure**: Docker Compose

## Deployment

Docker Compose on VPS, served at `novel.khtain.com`. PostgreSQL stores structured narratives; raw audio files stored on NAS via Tailscale for privacy.

## Outcome

BioWeaver has been used to produce several completed biographies. The voice-first approach proved essential — users who would never sit down to write a book found the experience of "telling stories" natural and enjoyable. The platform preserves family histories that would otherwise be lost.
