## Overview

FFT SiteReport digitizes the mandatory safety documentation workflow for solar installation teams. Before this tool, crews completed paper Fall Protection Plans (FPP) and Hazard Assessments (HA) — forms that were frequently lost, illegible, or incomplete. SiteReport replaces the entire paper process with a 6-step digital wizard accessible on any mobile device. Crew leads complete safety assessments on-site, capture e-signatures, and the system auto-archives everything to NAS storage with Telegram notifications to safety officers.

## Key Features

- **6-Step Safety Wizard**: Guided workflow that guarantees completeness. Steps: Site Info → Crew Roster → Hazard Identification → Control Measures → Sign-Off → Archive. Each step must be completed before proceeding.
- **E-Signature Capture**: Touch-screen signature capture on mobile devices. Legally valid digital signatures appended to PDF documents. Both crew lead and safety officer sign-off.
- **Employee & Certification Management**: Track crew members, their safety certifications (First Aid, Fall Protection, WHMIS), and expiry dates. Automatic alerts when certifications are due for renewal.
- **Groq Whisper AI Voice Transcription**: Voice-to-text for hazard notes and observations. Powered by Groq's ultra-fast Whisper implementation. Allows crew leads to dictate observations hands-free while wearing PPE.
- **Synology NAS Integration**: All completed safety documents are automatically archived to Synology NAS via FileStation API. Organized by date, site, and crew. Full-text searchable.
- **Telegram Bot Notifications**: Real-time alerts to safety officers when forms are submitted, when hazards are flagged as high-risk, and when certifications expire.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18, TypeScript, Vite 6, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion
- **Backend**: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy 2.0 Async, Pydantic v2
- **Database**: PostgreSQL 16
- **AI**: Groq Whisper (voice transcription), DeepSeek AI (hazard analysis)
- **Storage**: Synology NAS via FileStation API
- **Notifications**: Telegram Bot API
- **Infrastructure**: Docker Compose (3 services)

## Deployment

Docker Compose on VPS at `sitereport.khtain.com`. Three-container setup: frontend, backend API, PostgreSQL. NAS storage mounted via Tailscale for secure document archiving.

## Outcome

SiteReport eliminated paper safety forms across the solar installation fleet. Safety compliance rates improved from ~70% (paper forms often incomplete) to 100% (digital wizard enforces completeness). Voice transcription saves crews 15+ minutes per site.
